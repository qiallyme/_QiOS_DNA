# QiSystem Responsibilities

QiSystem is responsible for:

- Logs and operational records.
- Audits and validation outputs.
- Backup and restore references.
- Health checks and verification results.
- Generated reports.
- Generated indexes and inventories.
- Maintenance notes and cleanup tasks.

QiSystem is not responsible for:

- Runtime hosting details that belong in QiServer.
- App object models that belong in QiApp QiLife.
- Storage taxonomy that belongs in QiNexus.
- Doctrine that belongs in QiEOS.
