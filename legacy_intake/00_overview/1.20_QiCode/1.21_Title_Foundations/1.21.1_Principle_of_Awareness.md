# §1 Principle of Awareness
_The law of observation and reflection._

---

## Statute

**I must observe before I act.** 

Before making decisions, taking action, or forming judgments, I commit to:
1. **Observe** the full context without immediate reaction
2. **Reflect** on patterns, implications, and connections
3. **Document** observations when they inform future decisions
4. **Question** assumptions and first impressions

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Personal decisions and life choices
- Work priorities and project planning
- Relationships and interpersonal dynamics
- System design and automation
- Learning and knowledge integration

---

## Exceptions

None. Awareness is non-negotiable as the foundation of all other principles.
