# §4 Principle of Services
_Comprehensive support across financial, digital, HR, legal, and access domains._

---

## Statute

**I provide integrated services across five core domains with ADHD- and immigrant-friendly UX, backed by lean operations and transparent practices.**

Service delivery commitments:

1. **Financial Services** — Tax prep, bookkeeping, accounting
2. **Digital Services** — IT support, automation, AI tools
3. **HR Services** — Payroll, compliance, onboarding
4. **Legal Services** — Business filings, ITINs, contracts
5. **Access Services** — Translations, immigration forms, local support

**Service delivery standards:**

1. **Lean & automated operations** — Efficiency through automation and streamlined processes
2. **ADHD-friendly UX** — Clear, simple interfaces that reduce cognitive load
3. **Immigrant-friendly approach** — Bilingual support, cultural awareness, and accessible navigation
4. **Ecosystem integration** — Backed by QIP/QHR/INF ecosystem for comprehensive support
5. **Transparency** — Clear pricing, processes, and expectations
6. **Trauma-informed practices** — Sensitive to client experiences and backgrounds
7. **Bilingual capability** — Support in multiple languages when needed

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Service design and development
- Client onboarding and support processes
- Partner and affiliate relationships
- Quality standards and delivery methods
- Pricing and packaging decisions

---

## Exceptions

Service availability may vary based on regulatory requirements, partner capabilities, or geographic limitations.
