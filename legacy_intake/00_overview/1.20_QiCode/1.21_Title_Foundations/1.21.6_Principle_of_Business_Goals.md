# §6 Principle of Business Goals
_Measurable commitments to client success and sustainable operations._

---

## Statute

**I commit to achieving and maintaining specific goals that ensure client success and business sustainability.**

Goal commitments:

1. **80%+ membership renewals** — Maintain high retention through value delivery
2. **4.8+ satisfaction rating** — Deliver exceptional client experiences consistently
3. **$10K+/mo sustainable revenue** — Achieve financial sustainability for long-term operation
4. **Clear audit wins** — Demonstrate measurable value through successful outcomes
5. **Growth outcomes** — Enable measurable client growth and success

These goals are measured quarterly and reviewed annually.

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Service delivery quality standards
- Client relationship management
- Business planning and strategy
- Performance measurement and accountability
- Resource allocation decisions

---

## Exceptions

Goals may be adjusted during market shifts, major business model changes, or external factors beyond control, with documented rationale.
