# §3 Principle of Target Market
_Focus on those who most need accessible, trustworthy support._

---

## Statute

**I serve small businesses, solopreneurs, immigrants, and multilingual families seeking low-cost, trustworthy help with taxes, tech, forms, and growth.**

This principle defines who I commit to serving and why. I commit to:

1. **Prioritize accessibility** — Make services affordable and approachable for those with limited resources
2. **Build trust** — Establish credibility through transparency, trauma-informed practices, and consistent delivery
3. **Serve underserved communities** — Focus on immigrants, multilingual families, and small businesses who need support the most
4. **Remove barriers** — Address taxes, tech, forms, and growth challenges that create friction for these communities
5. **Cultural competence** — Provide bilingual, culturally-aware support that respects diverse backgrounds

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Business development and marketing decisions
- Service pricing and packaging
- Client selection and focus
- Content creation and messaging
- Partnership and referral strategies
- Product development priorities

---

## Exceptions

None. This target market focus is foundational to the mission.
