# §7 Principle of Organizational Structure
_Entity structure, domain, and affiliates under the hummingbird symbol._

---

## Statute

**I operate as Innovaflow Solutions LLC dba QiAlly, with defined domain, affiliates, and symbolic identity.**

Organizational commitments:

1. **Legal entity** — Innovaflow Solutions LLC doing business as QiAlly
2. **Primary domain** — [qially.me](https://qially.me/) as the main digital presence
3. **Affiliate ecosystem** — QIP, QHR LLP as affiliated entities providing integrated support
4. **Symbolic identity** — Hummingbird (walela) representing energy, speed, and precision
5. **Cohesive branding** — All entities operate under aligned mission and values

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Legal structure and compliance
- Branding and identity decisions
- Domain and digital presence
- Affiliate relationships and integration
- Entity registration and filings

---

## Exceptions

Affiliate relationships may evolve, but core entity structure remains stable.
