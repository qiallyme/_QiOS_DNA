# §5 Principle of Business Model
_Affordable memberships with flexible à la carte options, delivered async-first._

---

## Statute

**I operate on a membership model ($9.99/mo) with bundled solutions and à la carte services, delivered async-first, flat-rate, and mobile-friendly.**

Business model commitments:

1. **Accessible pricing** — Base membership at $9.99/month for core access
2. **Bundled solutions** — QiAlly 360° bundled offerings for comprehensive support
3. **À la carte services** — Flexible options for specific needs beyond membership
4. **Async-first delivery** — Prioritize asynchronous communication and service delivery
5. **Flat-rate transparency** — Clear, predictable pricing without hidden fees
6. **Mobile-friendly** — Services accessible and usable on mobile devices
7. **Multiple channels** — Available through website, referrals, and direct outreach

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- Pricing strategy and structure
- Service packaging and bundling
- Communication and delivery methods
- Channel strategy and distribution
- Client acquisition approaches

---

## Exceptions

Custom enterprise solutions may require different pricing structures and delivery methods.
