# §1 Principle of Social Media Guidelines
_Authentic connection, clear boundaries, and intentional presence online._

---

## Statute

**I engage on social media with intention, authenticity, and respect for boundaries.**

Social media engagement is an extension of real-world relationships. I commit to:

1. **Authenticity first** — Share truth, not persona. Present work and thoughts as they are, without artificial polish or false humility.
2. **Clear boundaries** — Separate personal, business, and client communication channels. Respect others' boundaries and honor my own.
3. **Value over volume** — Post when I have something meaningful to share, not from obligation or habit. Quality over frequency.
4. **Responsive engagement** — Acknowledge direct messages and meaningful interactions within 48 hours when possible, but never from a place of obligation.
5. **No comparison spirals** — Use social media as a tool for connection and learning, not for measuring worth against others' highlight reels.
6. **Credit and attribution** — Always credit original sources, ideas, and collaborators. Link generously to others' work.
7. **Privacy protection** — Protect client information, private conversations, and personal boundaries. Never share without explicit consent.
8. **Constructive discourse** — Engage in disagreement with curiosity, not combat. Disengage when conversations become harmful or unproductive.
9. **Platform-specific norms** — Respect each platform's culture and purpose. LinkedIn for professional discourse, Twitter/X for quick thoughts, GitHub for technical collaboration.
10. **Regular audit** — Review and prune accounts quarterly. Unfollow what no longer serves. Archive or delete outdated content when appropriate.

---

## Application

This principle applies to:
- All social media platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, GitHub, email communication)
- Personal accounts and professional profiles
- Client interaction on social platforms
- Content creation and sharing
- Direct messaging and public replies
- Platform presence and profile management
- Cross-posting between platforms

---

## Exceptions

- Client work may require specific platform engagement protocols that supersede general guidelines
- Emergency or time-sensitive communication may override normal response timelines
- Personal accounts may have more relaxed engagement patterns than professional accounts
