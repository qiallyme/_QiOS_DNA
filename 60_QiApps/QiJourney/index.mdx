# QiJourney

QiJourney is the interpretation, reflection, narrative, and creative branch of QiOS.

## Role

QiJourney turns canonical records into derived outputs:

- reflections
- narrative arcs
- synthesis
- creative exports
- journey maps
- story structure

## Boundary

QiJourney is derived from canonical records. It should link back to QiLife, QiNexus, or other source material when possible.
