# QiNexus Structure

Canonical bucket structure:

- `00 Inbox`: raw intake waiting for triage.
- `10 Workbench`: active working material.
- `20 Timeline`: date-ordered chronology.
- `30 Life`: personal and household domains.
- `40 People`: people-centered records.
- `50 Business`: business operations and projects.
- `60 Finance`: money, taxes, and financial records.
- `70 Legal`: legal matters and formal records.
- `80 Tech`: technical systems and software operations.
- `90 Assets`: durable asset records.
- `100 Data`: structured datasets and exports.
- `110 Reference`: reusable reference material.
- `900 Archive`: historical records.
- `990 System`: app logs, indexes, and manifests.
